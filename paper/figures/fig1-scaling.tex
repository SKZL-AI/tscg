\caption{Accuracy vs.\ tool catalog size for four small language models under natural (left) and TSCG-compressed (right) conditions on BFCL without multi-turn. Natural schemas expose severe scaling fragility: Phi-4 collapses to 0\% beyond 3~tools, Mistral drops from 83.5\% to 30\%, and Gemma degrades monotonically. Under TSCG compression, all four models maintain stable accuracy across the full 3--50 tool range, with Phi-4 recovering to ${\sim}$85--90\% and Gemma sustaining ${\sim}$65--87\%. Dashed lines indicate natural; solid lines indicate TSCG.}
\label{fig:scaling}
