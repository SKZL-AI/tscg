\caption{Rate--distortion trade-off for tool-schema compression. The $x$-axis measures token savings (higher = more compression); the $y$-axis measures downstream accuracy (higher = less distortion). The dashed line traces the Pareto frontier from the uncompressed Natural baseline to TSCG (74.8\% savings, 93.3\% accuracy). LLMLingua-2 falls well below this frontier: it achieves only 50.8\% savings at 80.0\% accuracy, meaning TSCG simultaneously saves \emph{more} tokens \emph{and} preserves higher accuracy. Compounding both methods (90.6\% savings) collapses accuracy to 0\%, confirming that post-hoc token pruning is incompatible with TSCG's structural encoding.}
\label{fig:rate-distortion}
